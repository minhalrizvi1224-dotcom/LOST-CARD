Refund Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026  ·  LOST CARD by S. M. Minhal Abbas Rizvi

We want you to be satisfied with LOST CARD. If you are not happy with your purchase, please read the policy below or contact us — we will do our best to resolve your issue fairly.

1. Subscription Plans

LOST CARD offers the following paid subscription plans:

  • 15-Day Access — one-time payment, no auto-renewal
  • Monthly Subscription — billed every 30 days, cancel anytime
  • Annual Subscription — billed once per year, cancel anytime

All payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited, who acts as the Merchant of Record for all transactions.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

You may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase if:

  • The AI features were completely inaccessible due to a technical fault on our side.
  • You were charged incorrectly or more than once for the same plan.
  • You did not receive the subscription access you paid for.

Refund requests made after 7 days from the purchase date are reviewed on a case-by-case basis at our discretion.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds are not provided in the following situations:

  • You changed your mind after using the AI features.
  • You forgot to cancel before the renewal date.
  • Your account was suspended due to a violation of our Terms of Service.
  • You have used a significant portion of the subscription period (more than 3 days for a 15-day plan, or more than 7 days for monthly/annual plans).
  • Technical issues caused by your own device, browser, or internet connection.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at minhalrizvi1224@gmail.com with:

  • The email address used for your LOST CARD account
  • Your Paddle order number or transaction ID (found in your payment confirmation email)
  • The reason for your refund request

We will respond within 3 business days. If approved, refunds are processed by Paddle and typically appear within 5–10 business days depending on your payment method.

5. Cancellations

You may cancel your Monthly or Annual subscription at any time through the Settings page in the App or by contacting us. Cancellation stops future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period. Access continues until the end of the paid period.

6. Disputes

If you believe a charge was made in error, please contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank. We process legitimate refund requests promptly and prefer to resolve issues directly. Unjustified chargebacks may result in account suspension.

7. Contact

For refund requests or billing questions, contact us at: minhalrizvi1224@gmail.com
