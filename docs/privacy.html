Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026  ·  LOST CARD by S. M. Minhal Abbas Rizvi

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how LOST CARD ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our psychological simulation web application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Email address and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Usage data: Simulation session records, move choices, neurological load scores, and AI conversation logs generated during your use of the App.
  • Payment information: Subscription plan status. Payment transactions are processed by Paddle; we do not store credit card numbers or billing details.
  • Technical data: Browser type, device type, and approximate region (for analytics and abuse prevention only).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App's simulation and AI features.
  • To track your subscription status and enforce free-tier limits.
  • To generate psychological analysis reports within your session.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, spam, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • To improve the App based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns.

4. Data Storage

Your account and session data are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted on Google's secure cloud infrastructure. Data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. AI Conversations

When you use the AI-powered simulation or custom chat features, your input is sent to the Groq API (or an equivalent AI provider) for processing. These messages are not stored permanently by the AI provider beyond the current session. We do not use your conversation content to train AI models.

6. Payments

Subscription payments are processed by Paddle.com Market Limited ("Paddle"), who acts as the Merchant of Record. Paddle's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment and billing data. We receive only your subscription status confirmation. View Paddle's privacy policy at paddle.com/legal/privacy.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service providers (Firebase, Groq, Paddle) necessary to operate the App.
  • When required by law or to protect against fraud or abuse.

8. Cookies

The App uses browser localStorage and sessionStorage to maintain your login state and preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

9. Children's Privacy

LOST CARD is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us for removal.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. Account deletion removes all associated session data within 30 days.

11. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), Firebase security rules, and hashed passwords. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
